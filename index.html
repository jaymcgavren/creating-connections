About Christine

My therapeutic approach is eclectic and recognizes that mental health does not exist in isolation but is influenced by societal, cultural, and political factors. I value collaboration and strive to build an egalitarian therapeutic relationship where your lived experience is honored and respected. Together, we will explore your strengths, identities, values, and goals while working toward meaningful and lasting change.

I have been helping individuals, couples, and families navigate life's challenges while reconnecting with their most authentic selves for over ten years. I believe that too often people receive messages—whether directly or indirectly—that they must hide parts of who they are in order to belong, be accepted, or maintain important relationships. My hope is to create a therapeutic space where you feel empowered to show up exactly as you are.

I draw from a variety of evidence-based approaches tailoring treatment to meet your unique needs. I am flexible, compassionate, and focused on helping you build insight, resilience, and a stronger connection with yourself and others.

I look forward to supporting you on your journey toward greater authenticity, connection, and well-being!

What to Expect

Taking your first step in healing can be hard and I am here to help make that easier for you. It is normal to be apprehensive of what to expect, especially if this is your first time in therapy. Therapy is a partnership that can take time to grow and build with your therapist, and that is okay. Some people come into therapy feeling like they don’t know where to start. I am here to guide you through the process. Your first session is an opportunity to “get to know you.” I will collect more comprehensive information about what your past and present experiences have been so that we can create goals and design a path to the future that you want.

Keep in mind that therapy is a process that can take time and is not an instant fix. By attending sessions regularly and being dedicated to implementing skills/insights learned during your sessions you can achieve therapeutic goals more efficiently.
