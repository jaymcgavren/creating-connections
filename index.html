I am dedicated to providing a safe, supportive, and nurturing environment where you can reconnect with yourself and build stronger, healthier relationships. I take a holistic approach to healing and recognize that emotional, physical, and existential wellness are deeply interconnected.

By integrating a variety of therapeutic approaches, we address the whole person and the unique experiences that have shaped your journey. Together, through honoring your strengths, we will work toward restoring balance and creating and fostering meaningful connections. I am here to help.

About Christine

For over ten years, I have helped individuals, couples, and families navigate life's challenges while reconnecting with their most authentic selves.

My therapeutic approach is grounded in Feminist Theory and draws from evidence-based practices including Person-Centered Therapy, Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Gottman Method Couples Therapy, Mindfulness Approaches, and Motivational Interviewing.

I strive to create a collaborative therapeutic relationship where your lived experiences are honored and respected.

When I'm not working with clients, I enjoy reading, watching television and movies, listening to music, and performing music.

What to Expect

Taking your first step in healing can be difficult, and it is normal to feel uncertain.

Therapy is a collaborative partnership that develops over time. Your first session is an opportunity for us to get to know one another and create a path toward the future you want.

Meaningful change takes time. Regular attendance and practicing skills learned during sessions can help you make steady progress toward your goals.
